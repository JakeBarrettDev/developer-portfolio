# Hello, World!

Welcome to my blog. I built this portfolio site with Next.js and I'm planning to use this space to write about things I'm learning, projects I'm building, and whatever else catches my interest.

## Why a blog?

Writing is thinking. When I try to explain something I've learned, I find out pretty quickly whether I actually understand it or not. Plus, it's a nice way to document the journey.

## What to expect

- Deep dives into projects I'm working on
- Things I learn about web development
- Occasional non-code musings

Stay tuned!
